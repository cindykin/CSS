background-image

  • digunakan utk membuat gambar menjadi background suatu elemen.
  • background of an element is the total size of the element, including padding and border (but not the margin)
  • a background-image is placed at the top-left corner of an element, and repeated both vertically and horizontally
  • bisa dua/lebih, kek png diatas jpg
  • valuenya : url("nama-gambar.jpg")
  • cara pakai : 

        body {
            background-image: url("nama-gambar.jpg"), url("opsional.png");
        }
